About Me

MATTHEW FLIGIEL

Data Scientist

As a data scientist, I aim to work on a variety of different projects with different aims, with a focus on making sure that all models and data structures I build are actually usable. I enjoy working across the data science pipeline - from setting up an environment to work in through data engineering, modeling, and deployment!

A proud Chicagoan, I graduated from Northwestern with a degree in Statistics and Biology in 2016, and completed my Master's of Science in Analytics in the summer of 2021. I like to spend my time exploring new places, cooking, listening to music, reading on current events, and biking.
